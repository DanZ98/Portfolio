    QUESTIONS?

    HOW TO BOOK A PHOTO SHOOT

    You can book via Phone, or by clicking on the "BOOK NOW" button and send me a booking request, a response will be emailed to you within 24 hours.  Please note that a $50 deposit is required to hold your requested booking date.

    HOW DO I PAY

    Full payment is due at the time of the shoot minus the deposit.  You can pay on this site with PayPal or with cash or money order.

    WHEN DO I NEED TO BOOK MY SHOOT

    It's best to schedule your shoot 2-3 days in advance.  This will help insure that you get the date that you need.  I will do my best to make sure we get the date you want for your shoot.

    HOW MANY PHOTO WILL BE PROVIDED

    I offer at least 20-35 high - resolution images for each shoot.

    HOW WILL I RECEIVE THE PHOTOS

    The images will be sent to you in a link via Email for download.  It will contain both MLS format files and high resolution files.

    CAN I CANCEL OR RESCHEDULE A SHOOT

    Yes, you may cancel or reschedule your shoot, but notice is required 48 hours prior to your booking date.

    CAN I MAKE A BOOKING DURING WEEKEND


    Yes, I am available to schedule a shoot 7 days a week, unless a prior booking is already been made.

    WHEN WILL I RECEIVE THE PHOTOS

    You will receive your images in two to three business days.

    HOW TO PREPARE THE PROPERTY BEFORE SHOOT

    The best way to prepare for your shoot is to imagine you're having guests over for a party.  You want to make your home feel warm and welcoming and most importantly attractive.  A lovely atmosphere will attract perspective buyers and help them to imagine themselves in the space.

    QUICK GUIDE FOR HOME PREPARATION


    INTERIOR

    • TURN ON ALL INTERIOR LIGHTS
    • PROTECT YOUR PRIVACY BY REMOVING PERSONAL ITEMS
    • CLEAR OFF COUNTER TOP
    • MAKE ALL BEDS
    • TUCK AWAY THE TRASH CAN

    EXTERIOR

    • Keep your car away from the drive away and front of the house
    • Remove garden tools and supplies
    • Make sure the garbage cans are not visible



    WHY HIRE A PROFESSIONAL FOR REAL ESTATE PHOTOS

    In our frowning market place, visual images have begun to dominate the internet.  Simply, a great image will help sell your home.  A beautiful image will catch the eye of your perspective buyer and set your space apart.  A strong digital image can be shared on multiple platforms to promote your space and sell your home faster!
