ABOUT

I am a front end web | email developer and my goal is to create memorable websites and emails. I have worked with HTML5, CSS3 Flex and Grid, JavaScript(JQuery), Bootstrap, WordPress, Git, Photoshop and Sketch. I have developed numerous responsive emails for various clients such as: Xarelto, Invokana, Essilor, Simponi, DIRECTV, Canon and Fisher Price. WordPress maintenance for my current employer's websites are additional tasks that I am gaining experience in. I have a “front end” web development certification from General Assembly and I utilize my varied background in my web site design creations. I also enjoy collaborating in team efforts and I look forward to working with you.
